RACEKON INDUSTRIES PVT LTD aims to provide consumers with the highest quality products by assuring their performance, consistency, safety and value. Our commitment is rooted in our corporate values and is essential to our continued growth and success.

Quality has been the most important element of our business activities hence, we give utmost priority to the same. Since the inception of our company, we have never compromised with the quality element and left no stone unturned to offer flawless range to our valuable clients. We have established modern quality testing facility that is managed by our expert quality controllers who keep a close tab on the quality of our products. Our quality controllers make maximum use of the available machines and ensure optimum durability and reliability of our Sensors and Measurement Devices.

We focus on meeting comprehensive “RACEKON INDUSTRIES PVT.LTD. Quality Standards” in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of our products. We meet all government requirements and consumer expectations worldwide and maintain these high quality standards as we design and manufacture our products by the most efficient means possible.

We ensure that our products are affordable and available to a large number of consumers throughout the world. Being an ISO 9001:2015, & CE-certified company product quality and service has been paramount for us. We have maintained a proper quality-management system comprising various compliances across different stages of manufacturing of products, right from the initial stage of procurement of raw materials. Our commitment to quality is vital to all we do
